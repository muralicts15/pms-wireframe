About This Property
Welcome to this stunning modern apartment in the heart of Perth CBD. Positioned on Level 5 of the prestigious Heights Apartments complex, this spacious 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment offers breathtaking city views and an exceptional lifestyle just minutes from the best of Perth.

The open-plan living and dining area flows seamlessly onto a generous 12m² balcony, perfect for entertaining. The kitchen features stone benchtops, a dishwasher and premium appliances.

Both bedrooms feature built-in robes, the master includes a luxurious ensuite. The building offers resort-style amenities — heated pool, fully equipped gym, and secure intercom access. One secure basement car bay and storage cage B-12 are included. Easy access to public transport, restaurants, shopping and entertainment.
